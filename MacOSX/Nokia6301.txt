This is a iSync plugin for Nokia 6301.

Copy this *.phoneplugin bundle into $HOME/Library/PhonePlugins/ or
/Library/PhonePlugins/ to finish the installation. After this,
restart iSync.

This is a modified version of the plugin from
Klaus Kjeldsen at
http://klauskjeldsen.dk/2007/06/08/free-nokia-6300-isync-plugin/
